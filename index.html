Workshop: Sculpting (more) Time with Computers

23-24 May 2023, University of Amsterdam

Building on the momentum of the first gathering in London, the follow up workshop: Sculpting (more) Time with Computers, will take place in Amsterdam on 23-24 May 2024.

In this second edition, we aim to gather a select group of academics and industry professionals to spark interdisciplinary dialogue on computational approaches to time-based media, with a focus on moving images and audiovisual culture. The objectives of the workshop are 1) to map ongoing research in the field, share data, methods, and approaches, and 2) collaboratively draft a road map for future activities with a view to integrate into a permanent research network.

Location: University of Amsterdam
