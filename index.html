Workshop: Sculpting (more) Time with Computers

23-24 May 2023, University of Amsterdam

Building on the momentum of the first gathering in London, the follow up workshop: “Sculpting (more) Time with Computers”, will take place in Amsterdam. This second workshop aims to gather a select group of 15-20 participants, encompassing academics, industry professionals, and students, for focused discussions and collaborative planning sessions. The goal is to spark interdisciplinary dialogue on computational approaches to time-based media, with a focus on moving images and audiovisual culture. We aim to map ongoing research in the field, share data, methods, and approaches, and collaboratively draft a road map for future activities with a view to integrate into a formal research network.
